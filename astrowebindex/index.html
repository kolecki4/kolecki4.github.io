What is the AstroWeb Index?

The AstroWeb Index is a way for me to pay forward all the helpful advice I've gotten over the years as a young astronomer. Often, the things you need are difficult to find, especially if you don't know exactly what you're looking for. I hope that this web directory will be helpful to career astronomers at every stage, as a convenient location for finding and discovering helpful tools and guidance.

This list is always going to be a work in progress. If you have suggestions for additions here, email me at jaredkolecki@gmail.com with a link and a short description of it and I'll do my best to categorize it and add it in.
