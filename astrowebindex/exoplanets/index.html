Resources For Early-Career Astronomers

Planet Detection

Lightkurve
A utility for downloading & analyzing light curves and target pixel files from Kepler, K-2, and TESS
(Python)
MulensModel
Modeling software for microlensing events
Poleski & Yi 2019(Python)
RVSearch
Uses models calculated by RadVel to search observations for periodic rv signals
Rosenthal et al. 2021 (Python)
Wotan
Contains dozens of algorithms for reducing, detrending, and analyzing light curve data
Hippke et al. 2019 (Python)

Dynamical Modeling

RadVel
Keplerian modeling for exoplanet characterization based on radial velocity data
Fulton et al. 2018 (Python)

Composition and Spectrum Modeling

EXOFASTv2
Powerful modeling software capable of modeling a planetary system based on input RV, transit, and astrometric data, as well as characterize the system's host star. Also offers a decent set of tutorials for new users
Eastman et al. 2019 (IDL)
petitRADTRANS
Software for computing transmission and emission spectra of exoplanets
Fulton et al. 2018 (Python)
