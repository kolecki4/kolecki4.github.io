About Me

My research intersects the booming fields of exoplanet characterization and galactic chemical evolution. I analyze spectra of planet-hosting stars to draw connections between the compositions of these stars, and the planets that orbit them. Outside of purely scientific endeavors, I've developed a passion for community building as a graduate student. For example, I helped refurbish the disused Notre Dame physics library into a community space for undergraduates and grad students alike. I can't possibly list all my interests and undertakings in just this short paragraph. So please, feel free to explore my projects and enjoy your stay in my little corner of the web.
